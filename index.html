El Paso

El Paso County

We flew into El Paso from Flagstaff, via Phoenix. Staying in downtown El Paso, we learned about the relationship between El Paso and Ciudad Juarez at the Museum of El Paso and Chamizal National Monument. We walked along the highway in a heatwave to find boot stores, and ended up with a second pair of botas para hombres from a Mexican boot store not far from El Centro shopping strip.

El Paso, Mexico and the US-Mexico border

El Paso Union Station

El Paso County

We walked from the hotel past the baseball stadium to the beautiful El Paso Union Station. We boarded the Texas Eagle to take us alongside the US-Mexico border then through the plains and desert of New Mexico and Arizona to Tucson. A few people encouraged us to buy burritos that were being sold on the El Paso platform (apparently the most delicious ever) but we decided not to, for fear of accidents in our pants if the train toilets were occupied!
